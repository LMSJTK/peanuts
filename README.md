## ğŸ® Core Gameplay

### Management
- Run a regional club that shares players, spectators, and resources with the league
- Set lineups, rotations, and strategic priorities
- Manage stadium economics:
  - Ticket prices
  - Concessions (yes, peanuts & crackerjacks)
  - Promotions, sponsorships, and marketing
- Develop your park over time, unlocking new advantages and abilities

### Simulation
- Every game is simulated **pitch by pitch**
- Outcomes are based on:
  - Pitcher stats
  - Batter stats
  - Defender stats
  - Situational context
  - Controlled randomness
- Full box scores and season-long stats are preserved
- You can:
  - Sim entire games
  - Watch games unfold live
  - Review detailed play-by-play logs

### Spectator Mode
- View games as a fan with:
  - Play-by-play text
  - Field visualization
  - Crowd reactions
- Stadium atmosphere and crowd energy matter

### Home Field Influence
- Each stadium has unique properties
- Large or energized crowds can temporarily alter reality:
  - Modifiers are applied *during simulation* without overwriting historical stats
  - Example: hitters performing better against a known starter at home
- Effects are subtle, situational, and designed to preserve realism

### Organ Player (Later Game)
- At higher progression levels, players may influence games indirectly
- The Organ is not cosmetic â€” it is infrastructure
- Timing, song choice, and momentum can amplify crowd effects
